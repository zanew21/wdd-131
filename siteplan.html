Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help others interested in Winders Funnel Cakes Catering and Event Booking. This website will provide contact information as well as the products that they sell and where they are located at the Eastern Idaho Fair.

Audience

The audience intended for this website will be those that own businesses or have other events coming up that want Winders Funnel Cakes to cater for.

Scenarios

How many flavors of funnel cakes do you have?

How can I book you guys for an event?

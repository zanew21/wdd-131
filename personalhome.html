Winder's Original Funnel Cakes is a Family Owned Business since 1985. What started as a way to support their kids through college, Tom and Ann Winder and their many children and grandchildren gather at the fair every year to hand out beautifully, hand-crafted funnelcakes. Whether you're a fair enthusiast or a first-time attendee, our funnel cakes are an experience not to be missed. Indulge your senses and satisfy your cravings with every delectable bite. It's the taste of tradition, served with a dash of excitement! So, why resist? Join us at the Eastern Idaho Fair and discover why our funnel cakes are the talk of the town!
